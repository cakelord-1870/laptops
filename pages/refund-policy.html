REFUND POLICY

LAPTOPS FOR LESS Inc. offers a 30-day return/exchange policy on all refurbished product unless otherwise specified.  Additional warranty is available.  Please contact us for more information.

To be eligible for a return, your item must be unused and in the same condition that you received it. It must also be in the original packaging (New, Manufacturer refurbished items).  Some restrictions apply.  Consumable goods such as printer toner, and paper, etc. are not eligible for return/exchange except in cases of manufacturer defect.  Gift cards and downloadable software are also not eligible for return or exchange.

Please do not send your purchase back to the original manufacturer.

REFUNDS

Once your return is received and inspected, you will receive an email confirmation.  The email will also confirm the status of your refund request. If approved, a refund will be processed, and a credit will automatically be applied to your credit card or original method of payment. 

Please note, all refunds are subject to a 25% re-stocking fee, unless otherwise specified.  RETURN SHIPPING CHARGES ARE THE RESPONSIBILITY OF THE CUSTOMER.  Discounted shipping rates are available through LAPTOPS FOR LESS Inc.  Please contact us for more information.

In the case of a product return, please note that original shipping costs are non-refundable.  The shipping cost will be deducted from your refund amount.

LATE/MISSING RETURNS (if applicable)

If you have not yet received your refund, please note it may take a few days before your refund is posted in your account.  For concerns, please contact us at support@buylaptops.ca.

EXCHANGES

Within the 30-day warranty period, if your item is defective or damaged, you may return it to us at no charge for an exchange. For exchange authorization, please contact us at support@buylaptops.ca for further instructions. Our return address is as follows: LAPTOPS FOR LESS Inc. 3356 Lakeshore Blvd W., Toronto, ON CA M8W 1M9.


SHIPPING

LAPTOPS FOR LESS Inc.  does NOT cover the cost of return shipments, unless the product is defective, or damaged.  Please ensure you contact us for further instructions before sending a return shipment for exchange or refund.   Discounted shipping rates are available through LAPTOPS FOR LESS Inc.  Please contact us for more information.  Returned product can be sent to the following address: LAPTOPS FOR LESS Inc., 3356 Lakeshore Blvd W., Toronto, ON CA M8W 1M9.
In the case of a product return, please note that the original shipping costs are non-refundable. If you receive a refund, the shipping charge will be deducted from your refund.

If you are shipping an item over $75.00, consider using a traceable shipping service, or purchasing shipping insurance. LAPTOPS FOR LESS Inc. can’t guarantee that your product will be returned.  Please contact us for further information about shipping options.  support@buylaptops.ca
